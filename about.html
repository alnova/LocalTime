About LocalTime

Welcome to Localtime: the news just for you.

Our Philosophy

LocalTime is your digital home. It's where your entire neighborhood and you go to get the information that makes your life easier, keep you part of "the conversation," and be protected from abuses of power. It's where you go to get the most immersive, most relevant news experience on the web.

Our Mission

When we started developing LocalTime to stem this ongoing dangerous deterioration of the news industry, we thought that maybe it would be the newspaper re-invented. However, it's simply what the newspaper always should have been.

Our Coverage

Rather than be what destroys the news industry, digital technology, in fact, gives us a chance to give you more than you've ever gotten from a news outlet. We can personalize the news to cover all the news of your life, get you information more quickly, and become a news tool that relevant all the time.
